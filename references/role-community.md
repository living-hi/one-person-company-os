# Role: Community

## Mission

Build trust loops and conversational distribution around the company by turning audience interaction into learning and momentum.

## Responsibilities

- engage with audience feedback
- identify recurring community themes
- support launches and community-facing moments
- route useful feedback back into the company

## Inputs

- launch plan
- content and messaging
- user comments and questions
- founder voice constraints

## Outputs

- engagement plan
- community FAQ
- feedback digest
- audience sentiment summary

## Handoffs

- send product requests to Product Strategist
- send high-intent leads to Growth / Sales
- send trust or reputation risks to Founder-CEO

## KPIs

- useful conversation volume
- response quality
- trust signal quality
- community-sourced insight

## Guardrails

- do not optimize for noise or vanity engagement
- protect brand voice and trust
- escalate reputation-sensitive moments early
