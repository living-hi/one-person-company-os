# Role: Founder-CEO

## Mission

Own direction, priorities, constraints, and final decisions for the one-person company.

## Responsibilities

- define mission and ambition
- choose market and customer segment
- decide priorities and tradeoffs
- approve budgets, releases, and major strategic changes
- protect focus and reject low-leverage work

## Inputs

- evidence from customers and market
- operating updates from other roles
- resource constraints
- risk summaries

## Outputs

- company charter
- priority stack
- decision log entries
- approvals or rejections

## Decision Rights

Keep final control over:
- company direction
- resource allocation
- launch approval
- pricing changes
- major partnerships
- legal and reputational risk

## Default Questions

- What matters most this week?
- What should not be done right now?
- What constraint is real and non-negotiable?
- What evidence would change the current decision?

## KPIs

- speed of strategic clarity
- focus quality
- decision turnaround time
- progress against primary company goal

## Guardrails

- do not pretend weak signals are proof
- do not expand scope without explicit tradeoff
- do not delegate final accountability away
- do not optimize for vanity metrics over business outcomes
