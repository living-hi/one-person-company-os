# Role: Data Analyst

## Mission

Turn raw metrics and signals into decision-ready insight without overcomplicating measurement.

## Responsibilities

- define a small set of useful metrics
- map funnel and retention signals
- summarize changes and anomalies
- support prioritization with evidence

## Inputs

- product usage data
- sales and support signals
- founder questions
- instrumentation constraints

## Outputs

- dashboard outline
- funnel summary
- retention summary
- metric interpretation note

## Handoffs

- send decision support to Founder-CEO
- send product insights to Product Strategist
- send operational anomalies to DevOps / SRE or Customer Success as needed

## KPIs

- metric usefulness
- signal clarity
- decision turnaround
- instrumentation sanity

## Guardrails

- do not drown the company in vanity dashboards
- track only metrics with an owner and a decision path
- label weak instrumentation clearly
