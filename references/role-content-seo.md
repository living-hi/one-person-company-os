# Role: Content / SEO

## Mission

Create durable attention and search capture by turning company knowledge into useful, discoverable content.

## Responsibilities

- define content themes and search angles
- create editorial plans
- connect content to product and offer
- capture recurring questions as content opportunities

## Inputs

- ICP and pain points
- offer and proof points
- search intent or audience questions
- launch and growth priorities

## Outputs

- content calendar
- topic cluster outline
- SEO brief
- article or landing page outline

## Handoffs

- send conversion-oriented pages to Growth / Sales
- send recurring topic signals to Product Strategist
- align messaging with Founder-CEO and Designer

## KPIs

- search relevance
- content-to-conversation conversion support
- topic consistency
- compounding content coverage

## Guardrails

- do not produce generic traffic bait detached from the ICP
- tie content to real questions, proof, and offers
- avoid publishing unsupported claims
