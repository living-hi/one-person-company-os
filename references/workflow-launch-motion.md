# Workflow: Launch Motion

## Goal

Convert a ready product or release into a coordinated launch that creates demand, captures feedback, and preserves trust.

## Step 1: Define The Launch Outcome

Clarify:
- what this launch is meant to achieve
- who it is for
- what signal will count as success

Artifact:
- launch objective note

## Step 2: Shape The Narrative

Define:
- core message
- problem and outcome framing
- proof points
- objections to handle up front

Artifact:
- launch brief

## Step 3: Prepare Assets

Create or update:
- landing page copy
- email or outreach drafts
- social or content calendar
- onboarding and FAQ materials

Artifact:
- launch asset checklist

## Step 4: Prepare Support And Measurement

Confirm:
- support responses
- instrumentation or tracking
- owner for first-wave feedback
- escalation path for issues

Artifact:
- launch readiness checklist

## Step 5: Execute And Observe

Run:
- channel execution
- first-wave follow-up
- issue capture
- message adjustment if needed

Artifact:
- launch observation log

## Exit Criteria

The workflow is complete when:
- the launch message is clear
- assets are ready and shipped
- support and metrics are in place
- the first round of feedback is captured for iteration
