# Artifact Templates

Use these structures directly in-context, or copy the matching files from `assets/templates/` with `scripts/init_company.py`.

## Company Charter

Use this when the company still lacks a sharp operating definition.

```md
# Company Charter

## Mission

## Target Customer

## Problem

## Offer

## Business Model

## Primary Goal This Quarter

## Constraints

## Non-Negotiables

## Current Bottleneck
```

## Role Card

Use this for each AI role that should have stable responsibilities.

```md
# Role: <Name>

## Mission

## Responsibilities

## Inputs

## Outputs

## Handoffs

## KPIs

## Guardrails
```
