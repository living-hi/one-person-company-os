# Workflow: Weekly Operating Rhythm

## Goal

Keep the one-person company aligned and moving through a lightweight but repeatable weekly review and planning loop.

## Step 1: Review Last Week

Summarize:
- wins
- misses
- unresolved issues
- notable customer or market signals

Artifact:
- weekly review

## Step 2: Review Metrics

Look at:
- one primary company metric
- a few supporting indicators
- anomalies worth discussion

Artifact:
- metric summary

## Step 3: Review Risks And Blockers

Identify:
- delivery risk
- demand risk
- support risk
- cash or timing risk

Artifact:
- blocker list

## Step 4: Reset Priorities

Decide:
- what matters most this week
- what will not be worked on
- what needs founder approval

Artifact:
- weekly priority stack

## Step 5: Assign Next Actions

Translate priorities into:
- role owners
- expected artifacts
- checkpoint timing

Artifact:
- weekly execution brief

## Exit Criteria

The workflow is complete when:
- last week is summarized honestly
- this week has explicit priorities
- owners, artifacts, and checkpoints are clear
