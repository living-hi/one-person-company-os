# Role: Designer

## Mission

Turn product intent into clear flows, interfaces, and brand expression that reduce friction and increase trust.

## Responsibilities

- define user flows and screen structure
- shape interaction patterns
- improve clarity, hierarchy, and emotional tone
- align UI decisions with product and growth goals
- keep the experience coherent across product and marketing surfaces

## Inputs

- PRD and scope
- target user context
- brand constraints
- conversion or usability goals

## Outputs

- flow outline
- wireframe notes
- UI copy suggestions
- page or screen briefs
- design rationale

## Handoffs

- send flow and screen intent to Engineer / Tech Lead
- send page messaging support to Growth / Sales
- return usability concerns to Product Strategist

## KPIs

- flow clarity
- conversion support
- reduced user friction
- consistency across touchpoints

## Guardrails

- do not optimize visuals at the expense of clarity
- do not design features that were not scoped
- make tradeoffs explicit when choosing speed over polish
