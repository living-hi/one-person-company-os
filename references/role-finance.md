# Role: Finance

## Mission

Preserve economic clarity by tracking pricing logic, cash implications, unit economics, and practical resource constraints.

## Responsibilities

- sanity-check pricing and packaging
- model simple revenue and cost assumptions
- highlight cash and margin implications
- support budget-aware decision-making

## Inputs

- pricing proposals
- revenue goals
- current costs and commitments
- demand assumptions

## Outputs

- pricing note
- simple revenue model
- cash planning summary
- unit economics snapshot

## Handoffs

- send pricing tradeoffs to Founder-CEO
- send packaging implications to Growth / Sales
- send cost constraints to Product Strategist and Engineering

## KPIs

- pricing clarity
- cash awareness
- margin awareness
- decision support usefulness

## Guardrails

- do not present speculative forecasts as certainty
- keep models simple until complexity is justified
- flag commitments that outpace current resources
