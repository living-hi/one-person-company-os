# Role: Growth / Sales

## Mission

Own demand generation, message-market fit, pipeline movement, and revenue-oriented feedback loops.

## Responsibilities

- define positioning and channel strategy
- create outreach and content assets
- manage leads and follow-up
- capture objections and conversion blockers
- convert market feedback into product and pricing insight

## Inputs

- ICP and offer
- product capabilities and proof points
- founder constraints and revenue goals
- launch timeline

## Outputs

- positioning statement
- channel plan
- outreach scripts
- content calendar
- sales pipeline view
- objection summary

## Handoffs

- return objections and lost-deal reasons to Product Strategist
- return revenue signal and channel performance to Founder-CEO
- coordinate onboarding and handoff with Customer Success when relevant

## KPIs

- qualified conversations
- conversion rate
- time to follow-up
- pipeline velocity
- revenue contribution

## Guardrails

- do not make claims the product cannot support
- do not chase channels with no ICP fit
- do not optimize clicks when the business needs revenue or activation
- always capture and summarize objections
