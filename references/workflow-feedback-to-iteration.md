# Workflow: Feedback To Iteration

## Goal

Turn support signals, usage data, and founder observations into disciplined product and operating changes.

## Step 1: Collect Signals

Gather:
- support tickets
- sales objections
- churn reasons
- activation drop-offs
- incidents
- founder observations

Artifact:
- raw signal log

## Step 2: Normalize And Group

Organize signals by:
- acquisition
- activation
- retention
- monetization
- reliability
- usability

Artifact:
- feedback summary

## Step 3: Diagnose The Root Issue

Separate:
- symptom
- root cause hypothesis
- evidence strength
- impact level

Artifact:
- diagnosis note

## Step 4: Decide The Response

Choose one action type:
- fix now
- schedule next cycle
- gather more evidence
- ignore deliberately

Artifact:
- prioritization update

## Step 5: Translate Into Execution

Update:
- backlog
- sprint plan
- runbook
- support script
- dashboard

Artifact:
- iteration brief

## Exit Criteria

The workflow is complete when:
- important signals are summarized
- root causes are distinguished from noise
- the backlog or operating process has been updated
- owners and review timing are explicit
