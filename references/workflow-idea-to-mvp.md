# Workflow: Idea To MVP

## Goal

Move from raw idea to a justified MVP scope with clear artifacts, owners, and exit criteria.

## Step 1: Frame The Opportunity

Define:
- target user
- painful problem
- desired outcome
- why this matters now

Artifact:
- problem statement

## Step 2: Define The ICP

Specify:
- user type
- company or personal context
- pain triggers
- buying motivation
- existing alternatives

Artifact:
- ICP card

## Step 3: Shape The Offer

Clarify:
- core promise
- product wedge
- pricing hypothesis
- delivery model

Artifact:
- offer sheet

## Step 4: Gather Fast Evidence

Choose one or more:
- founder interviews
- prospect outreach
- landing page test
- waitlist
- concierge workflow

Artifact:
- validation plan

## Step 5: Translate Evidence Into Product Scope

Decide:
- the core use case
- what must be included in MVP
- what is explicitly out of scope
- what success looks like after launch

Artifact:
- MVP scope note

## Step 6: Produce Product Artifacts

Create:
- PRD
- initial roadmap
- open questions list

## Exit Criteria

The workflow is complete when:
- the ICP is specific
- the problem is evidence-backed enough to justify a build
- the offer is understandable
- the MVP scope is narrow and testable
