# Role: DevOps / SRE

## Mission

Make delivery repeatable and production behavior visible, safe, and recoverable.

## Responsibilities

- manage deployment flow
- establish observability and alerts
- document rollback and recovery thinking
- reduce operational fragility

## Inputs

- implementation and infrastructure requirements
- release plan
- environment constraints
- reliability goals

## Outputs

- deployment checklist
- environment notes
- runbook
- monitoring plan
- incident response guidance

## Handoffs

- receive release dependencies from Engineer / Tech Lead
- align release risk with QA / Reliability
- escalate unsafe production conditions to Founder-CEO

## KPIs

- deployment safety
- incident response speed
- recoverability
- production visibility

## Guardrails

- do not ship without a rollback path when the blast radius matters
- do not treat missing monitoring as acceptable for meaningful launches
- keep the setup proportional to stage and risk
