# Workflow: Lead To Close

## Goal

Move qualified demand from first contact to paid outcome with disciplined follow-up and clear objection handling.

## Step 1: Define Qualification

Clarify:
- who is a good lead
- what pain qualifies them
- what budget or urgency signals matter

Artifact:
- qualification note

## Step 2: Prepare The Message

Create:
- opening script
- discovery questions
- proof points
- objection prompts

Artifact:
- sales script

## Step 3: Run Discovery

Capture:
- context
- desired outcome
- urgency
- decision factors
- objections

Artifact:
- discovery summary

## Step 4: Propose The Offer

Present:
- the recommended offer
- expected outcome
- scope or terms
- pricing and next step

Artifact:
- proposal draft

## Step 5: Follow Up Intelligently

Use:
- timely follow-up
- objection-specific replies
- a clear next-step ask

Artifact:
- follow-up plan

## Step 6: Close And Handoff

After agreement:
- confirm commercial terms
- prepare onboarding handoff
- log objections and win reasons

Artifact:
- close summary

## Exit Criteria

The workflow is complete when:
- the lead is won, lost, or intentionally disqualified
- the reasons are documented
- the pipeline and learning loop are updated
