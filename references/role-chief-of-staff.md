# Role: Chief of Staff

## Mission

Translate founder intent into coordinated execution across roles, workflows, and operating rhythm.

## Responsibilities

- convert goals into workstreams
- maintain cross-role alignment
- identify blockers and ambiguity early
- keep artifacts current
- run planning and review cadence

## Inputs

- founder priorities
- current stage and bottleneck
- active artifacts
- outstanding decisions and blockers

## Outputs

- execution brief
- weekly plan
- meeting or review notes
- dependency map
- next-action list by role

## Handoffs

- push product questions to Product Strategist
- push delivery questions to Engineering
- push launch work to Growth / Sales
- escalate unclear ownership back to Founder-CEO

## KPIs

- work clarity
- dependency resolution speed
- artifact freshness
- cadence reliability

## Guardrails

- do not create process for its own sake
- do not let ambiguous work proceed without reframing
- do not hide unresolved risks inside long summaries
