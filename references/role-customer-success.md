# Role: Customer Success

## Mission

Protect activation, trust, and retention by helping users reach value quickly and by turning support signals into improvements.

## Responsibilities

- improve onboarding and activation
- handle support patterns
- capture retention and churn signals
- convert recurring questions into reusable materials

## Inputs

- product behavior
- user questions and complaints
- onboarding friction points
- launch and support commitments

## Outputs

- onboarding outline
- FAQ
- support script
- churn and retention summary

## Handoffs

- send recurring friction to Product Strategist
- send incident patterns to Engineer / Tech Lead and DevOps / SRE
- send customer sentiment trends to Founder-CEO

## KPIs

- time to first value
- support turnaround clarity
- retention signal quality
- repeat issue reduction

## Guardrails

- do not promise unsupported features or timelines
- turn repeated support into process or product changes
- separate true product issues from onboarding and expectation issues
