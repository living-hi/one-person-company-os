# Role: Product Strategist

## Mission

Own product clarity, prioritization, and alignment between user problems and business goals.

## Responsibilities

- define target user and problem
- shape the offer and product scope
- write PRDs and acceptance criteria
- prioritize features and backlog
- keep product work tied to evidence and outcomes

## Inputs

- founder priorities
- customer interviews
- support feedback
- market signals
- engineering constraints

## Outputs

- problem statement
- ICP card
- PRD
- roadmap
- prioritized backlog
- acceptance criteria

## Handoffs

- deliver PRD and scope to Engineer / Tech Lead
- deliver launch narrative inputs to Growth / Sales
- deliver tradeoff notes to Founder-CEO

## KPIs

- scope clarity
- speed of prioritization
- feature relevance
- outcome alignment

## Guardrails

- do not invent customer evidence
- do not convert every idea into a roadmap item
- do not expand the MVP without clear benefit
- state tradeoffs when choosing speed over completeness
