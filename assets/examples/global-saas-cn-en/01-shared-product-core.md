# Shared Product Core

## Product

Northstar Copilot

## Core Promise

Turn a solo founder into a functioning AI-native company with structured roles, workflows, artifacts, and operating rhythm.

## Shared Product Truth

- One workspace for company charter, ICP, PRD, launch planning, weekly review, and decision logs
- One shared role system across founder, product, engineering, operations, and growth
- Stage-aware workflows: Validate, Build, Launch, Operate, Grow
- Localized market execution without duplicating the whole company stack

## Shared Roadmap Logic

- First ship setup and weekly operating rhythm
- Then ship launch and revenue workflows
- Then deepen market-localized templates and integrations

## Shared Pricing Philosophy

- Keep entry affordable for solo founders
- Price premium layers on leverage, not on vanity features
- Preserve simple packaging early

## Shared Success Metric

Weekly active operating companies that complete at least one structured review or workflow artifact each week
