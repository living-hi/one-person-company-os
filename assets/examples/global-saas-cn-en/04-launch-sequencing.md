# Launch Sequencing

## Shared Principle

Launch one company with one product core. Sequence markets deliberately.

## Recommended Sequence

1. Validate in the market with the shortest path to founder conversations.
2. Ship one strong workflow loop: setup plus weekly review.
3. Gather proof that users return beyond first-time setup.
4. Localize landing pages, examples, and outreach for the second priority market.
5. Keep shared artifacts and product truth synchronized.

## What Stays Shared

- product core
- role model
- lifecycle model
- weekly rhythm
- roadmap logic

## What Changes By Market

- landing page copy
- channel plan
- content calendar
- case studies
- objection handling
- onboarding examples

## Near-Term Success Criteria

- 10 high-quality founder conversations
- clear repeat-use signal
- at least one public example for each priority market
