# English Market Brief

## Market Role

Priority market with a large base of indie hackers, solo founders, and AI-native product builders.

## ICP

- solo SaaS founders
- indie hackers
- technical creators building AI products
- small founder-led teams seeking leverage without headcount

## Pain Framing

- "AI helps me execute tasks, but I still do all company coordination manually."
- "I need a founder OS, not just more agents."
- "I need product, launch, operations, and sales to work as one system."

## Trust Signals

- clear framework language
- realistic artifacts
- reusable templates
- stage-based execution
- evidence that the workflow is useful after day one

## Likely Channels

- GitHub
- X
- product communities
- indie hacker ecosystems
- founder newsletters

## Likely Objections

- "Why not just use ChatGPT with a custom prompt?"
- "Is this a system or just documentation?"
- "Will this help me run the business weekly, not just plan it?"

## Localization Notes

- Use direct, crisp, product-oriented language
- Emphasize systems thinking, operating cadence, and founder leverage
- Lead with repeatable outcomes rather than philosophical framing
