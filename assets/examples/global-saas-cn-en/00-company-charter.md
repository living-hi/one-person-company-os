# Northstar Copilot Company Charter

Created: 2026-04-01
Business model: saas

## Mission

Help solo founders and tiny product teams run lean AI-native businesses with less coordination overhead and faster execution.

## Target Customer

- Primary: solo founders building AI SaaS products
- Secondary: 2-10 person product teams that still operate like founder-led companies

## Problem

Founders can move fast with AI, but they still lack a coherent operating system for product, launch, operations, sales, and review cadence across markets.

## Offer

An AI-native operating layer that turns one founder into a working company with clear roles, artifacts, workflows, and weekly discipline.

## Business Model Notes

- Product-led SaaS
- Shared product core
- Chinese and English as first-class execution markets
- Self-serve entry with higher-value team and template packs later

## Primary Goal This Quarter

Validate demand with solo SaaS founders, ship the first usable workflow pack, and achieve repeatable onboarding into weekly operating use.

## Constraints

- One founder
- Limited budget
- Must keep product core shared across markets
- Launch sequencing must stay focused

## Non-Negotiables

- Real artifacts over vague advice
- One shared product truth
- Localized go-to-market where required
- Founder retains final approval rights

## Current Bottleneck

Message-market fit and proof that founders will repeatedly return for weekly operating workflows, not just initial setup.
