# Chinese Market Brief

## Market Role

Priority market with high density of AI-curious founders and strong appetite for leverage tooling.

## ICP

- Chinese-speaking solo founders
- Independent developers
- Small AI service or SaaS operators
- Builders using WeChat, Xiaohongshu, Bilibili, Douyin, or founder communities for acquisition

## Pain Framing

- "I can build, but I cannot run product, ops, sales, and delivery like a real company by myself."
- "I have tools, but no operating system."
- "AI helps me produce more, but not coordinate better."

## Trust Signals

- concrete templates
- visible execution examples
- realistic launch and revenue workflows
- bilingual/global optionality

## Likely Channels

- founder communities
- WeChat private network
- Xiaohongshu educational content
- Bilibili demos
- Chinese AI builder circles

## Likely Objections

- "Is this just a prompt bundle?"
- "Will this create more process instead of more leverage?"
- "Can it work with my existing tools and projects?"

## Localization Notes

- Use direct proof and operational outcomes
- Emphasize efficiency,闭环, and可执行性 in final messaging
- Prefer examples over abstract methodology
